You know that feeling you get

when nothing

seems good enough?

You try and try. You rewrite, you erase and redo... but the feeling persists

It's like a weight...

Your stomach folds and unfolds, your heart feels so so tight...
But everyone encourages you.
Maybe you’re imagining things?
Blowing everything out of proportion?
I still hate everything!
And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...And then...
What was the point?
I forgot...
Why was I doing this?
What was I doing again?
But eventually... It’s just...

NOTHING

EMPTY

VOID

Who am I?

What am I?

What am I? What am I? What am I? What am I?

No one cares... No one really wants to hear it... No one really cares...

MOTIVATION, ADVISES, REASONS, FRIENDS, FAMILY, PURPOSE, INTENTION, EMOTIONS, APPROVAL, STATUS, SUCCESS, MONEY, CONFORT...

The meaning bounces and mixes... it gets lost in space.

Until...

Someday...

The weight... The tightness...

It's no longer there.

Something...someone...A color... A phrase... A memory...A melody...








...makes you try again.
